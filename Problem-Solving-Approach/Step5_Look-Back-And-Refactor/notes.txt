Look Back And Refactor  
    

    Refactoring Questions
        . Can you check the result?

        . Can you derive the result differently?

        . Can you understand it at a glance? 
            -how intuitive is you solution?

        . Can you use the result or method for some other problem?
            - Any similarities between the problem you're solving and other problems you've solved
            
        . Can you improve the performance of you solution?
            - How can you make it perform better? (time complexity space complexity)

        . Can you think of other ways to refactor?
            - If you're working for a company , does it following company guidelines? language standards?

        . How have other people solved this problem?
